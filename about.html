About


Our Story
Most people will not understand that our heritage dates back to 2013.
In 2020, we adopted a new focus to create a new hybrid network digital
office specification designed specifically for software development and
publishing industries working with sensitive data.
In 2021, Harmonious Platform Systems was registered as a business.


Our Founder
We operate from the Gold Coast, Australia.
Our founder, Chris McGimpsey-Jones, along with a small group of dedicated
volunteers in various locations around the world, all contribute to the
operations of a multi-domain secure siloed intranet which interconnects
seamlessly with the global internet.
Chris is also the administrator for the Freedom Publishers Union website.
