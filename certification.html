Certification


Harmonious Platform Systems operates an internal certification program for software where certificiation is provided based on our internal assessment of usability, reliability and security.

We can only certify a small selection of software. However, if specific software is not certified for the Harmonious Platform it does not mean it is incompatible or that we do not support its use with the platform. It simply means that we have not yet performed the required amount of testing for it to be certified and officially endorsed by Harmonious Platform Systems.
