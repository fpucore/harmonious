Certifications
Certificate Level 1 (HPSA1NA) - NETWORK ADMINISTRATION
  Level 1 certifies a proven ability in Network Administration of an operational computer network, functioning as part of the Harmonious Platform Systems infrastructure.

Certificate Level 2 (HPSA2EN) - ENGINEER
  Level 2 certifies a proven ability as an Engineer to resolve issues without impacting data or security integrity of an operational computer network, functioning as part of the Harmonious Platform Systems infrastructure.

Certificate Level 3 (HPSA3AR) - ARCHITECT
  Level 3 certifies a proven ability as an Architect to develop and implement new strategies and technologies for computer networks, functioning as part of the Harmonious Platform Systems infrastructure.

Certificate Level 4 (HPSA4MA) - MASTER
  Level 4 certifies a proven ability as a Master to manage computer systems and servers with unrestricted administrative access and to make exceptional decisions for computer networks, functioning as part of the Harmonious Platform Systems infrastructure.

Certificate Level 5 (HPSA5IN) - INSTRUCTOR (In)
  Level 5 certifies a proven ability as an Instructor in Levels 1-4 to instruct and mentor students participating in academic activities, formed as part of the Harmonious Platform Systems Academy.

Certificate Level 6 (HPSA6PR) - PROFESSIONAL (Pr)
  Level 6 certifies a proven ability as a Professional in Levels 1-5 to qualify for entry to the Board and be formally recognized as a Harmonious Platform Systems Academy Board Member.
