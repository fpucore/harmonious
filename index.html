Harmonious Platform Systems is a Gold Coast-based startup. 🌴

News

February 10, 2022

We have completed our investigation into a recent security breach that we detected.
The breach has now been contained and all systems on Harmonious Platform Systems network are now secure.
The breach was not as severe as we anticipated and was limited to one web browser being hijacked on the Mainframe.
The web browser has been returned to default settings and reconfigured successfully, eliminating the threat.
As a precaution, our proxy server has been assessed and traffic analyzed.
The proxy server was not affected.
This investigation is now closed.

February 8, 2022

***** IMPORTANT! *****
We are currently investigating a security breach which has penetrated our network.
Our initial assessment points to a Mainframe breach.
This is both good and bad.
The good news is it means that it may be limited to one system and can be restricted to the intranet.
The bad news is it makes it more difficult to investigate the entry point the breach has occurred.
More information will be posted to the website when we complete our investigation.
